## 3. Revenue Sharing

### 3.1 Eligibility

You are eligible for revenue sharing if Your Contribution meets the following criteria:

| Contribution Type | Minimum Threshold | Revenue Share |
|-------------------|-------------------|---------------|
| Core Feature Development | 200+ lines of code, accepted | 5-10% of feature-related revenue |
| Major Bug Fix | Critical bug, 50+ lines | One-time bonus: $50-500 |
| Documentation | Significant documentation | One-time bonus: $20-100 |
| Minor Contributions | < 50 lines, small fixes | Recognition only |

### 3.2 Revenue Share Calculation

For ongoing revenue share contributions:

```
Your Revenue Share = (Attributed Revenue) × (Your Share Percentage)

Where:
- Attributed Revenue = Revenue directly attributable to feature you contributed
- Your Share Percentage = Agreed percentage based on contribution significance
```

### 3.3 Terms

| Term | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Duration** | 2 years from contribution merge date |
| **Minimum Payout** | $100 USD (accumulated across payments) |
| **Payment Frequency** | Quarterly (if threshold met) |
| **Payment Methods** | PayPal, Bank Transfer, Crypto (USDC/ETH) |
| **Reporting** | Quarterly revenue report for your contributions |

### 3.4 Revenue Share Agreement

For contributions eligible for revenue sharing, a separate Revenue Share Addendum will be signed specifying:

1. The specific contribution(s) covered
2. The revenue share percentage
3. The attribution methodology
4. Payment details

### 3.5 No Guarantee

Revenue sharing is contingent upon:
- Commercial success of the Project
- Continued relevance of Your Contribution
- Accurate attribution being possible

We do not guarantee any minimum payment amount.
